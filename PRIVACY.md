# PlayFunded Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-04

## 1. Data We Collect

- Account data (name, email, auth identifiers).
- Activity data (challenge activity, picks, platform interactions).
- Payment-related records (transaction IDs, status, provider metadata).
- Compliance data where required (for example KYC submissions and payout checks).

## 2. Why We Use Data

- To provide the service and user accounts.
- To process payments and payouts.
- To prevent fraud and abuse.
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
- To provide support and service operations.

## 3. Legal Basis

Depending on region, processing is based on contract performance, legal obligations, legitimate interests, and consent where required.

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers required to operate the product, such as hosting, authentication, payment, and communications providers.

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for service delivery, legal obligations, dispute handling, and security purposes.

## 6. Security

We apply technical and organizational controls to protect user data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 7. Your Rights

Where applicable law grants rights, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data.

## 8. Cross-Border Processing

Data may be processed in countries different from your own, subject to applicable safeguards.

## 9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be communicated in-product or by other reasonable means.

## 10. Contact

Privacy and data requests: `support@playfunded.lat`
